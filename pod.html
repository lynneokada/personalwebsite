Pod Damned



Pod Damned is a short Oculus experience.

You are prematurely awakened from deep space hypersleep. What do you do now?

The player must follow a set of instructions that involve looking at bottons to move on. This requires the Oculus Rift's line of sight to collide with the 3D environment in Unity. After all button triggers go off the door opens to the next room to allow the player to look through a window into outer space.

Oculus Rift experience created for the 2015 Global Game Jam. This experience was created with Unity3D and the Oculus DK2. Worked on by 2 developers (Rudy Gomez, me) and 2 artists (Kat Valliant and Paul Yula).

Music composed by my good friend, Aiden McKee.
