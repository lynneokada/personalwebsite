MenMa



* RaMenMap: menma is a well known ingredient for ramen.

My love for ramen is very strong. üçúüçúüçú

Started at the WatchKit Hackathon in November 2015 Keita Ito, a fellow iOS developer friend of mine, developed this app through our common obsession. We are building a Apple Watch app that gathers the 5 closest ramen restaurants near you with a single tap.

We are using Swift 2 and WatchOS 2 to create Menma, along with Foursquare API to gather distance, price, menu, and open hours.

With the updates on WatchOS 2 connectivity between watch and phone has improved.
